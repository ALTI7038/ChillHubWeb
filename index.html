ℹ️ ChillHub About & FAQ

❓ About ChillHub

The original ChillHub Community was founded in 2025. Unfortunately, it was heavily raided, making it impossible to continue safely. Instead of giving up, we rebuilt the community from the ground up with stronger security and a fresh start with ChillHub SecEra of 2026 where your journey starts here.

❓ ChillHub FAQ

ChillHub Community is a multi-purpose Discord community where you can have fun, make new friends, and learn through our different societies in a safe and welcoming environment.
Open a ticket in our Support/Ticket channel first. Our staff will then provide you with the official Report or Appeal form and guide you through the process.
You need to verify your account in our Verification channel first. Once verified, you'll be able to access the rest of the server.
Open a ticket and let our staff know you'd like to become a staff member. We'll provide you with the Staff Application Form to complete. All applications are reviewed before a decision is made.
We currently have three societies:
  • 🎮 Gaming Academy Society
  • 🎨 Art Academy Society
  • ♟️ Chess Academy Society
Each society has its own activities where you can learn, improve your skills, and meet people with similar interests.

📜 ChillHub Community Regulations

A | Rule Enforcement

A1 🌐 Server Compliance

  • You Must Follow All Server Regulations and Discord Terms of Service.
  • No Rule Bypassing or Exploiting Loopholes, Using Alt Accounts to Avoid Punishment, External Abuse Targeting This Server or Members.

A2 ⛔ Restricted Content

  • No Inappropriate, Prohibited, Harmful and Unauthorized Content allowed.
  • No NSFW or Adult Content, Hate Speech or Discrimination, Violence or Gore Content, Illegal Content, Malware or Harmful Files.

A3 💬 Chat Conduct

  • You Must Keep Conversations Respectful and Use the Proper Channels.
  • No Spam or Flooding, Harassment or Toxic Behavior and No Excessive Profanity.

A4 🗣️ Voice Conduct

  • You Must Respect Everyone in Voice Channels and Keep Your Microphone Clear.
  • No Mic Spamming, Excessive Background Noise and No Interrupting or Talking Over Others.

A5 📣 Advertisement

  • No Unauthorized Advertising, Mass DM Advertising, Server Invite Spam.
  • No Self-Promotion or Commercial Promotions without Permission.

A6 🛡 Security & Privacy

  • You Must Protect Your Personal Information and Report Suspicious Activity.
  • No Scams or Phishing, Sharing Malware or Malicious Files/Link and No Doxxing or Privacy Violations.

A7 🛠 Staff & Authority

  • You Must Respect Staff Members and Follow Staff Instructions.
  • No Staff Impersonation, Harassment or Abuse and No Bypassing Staff Decisions.

A8 🏅 Event Conduct

  • You Must Follow the Event Rules and Respect the Event Host.
  • No Cheating or Exploiting, Disrupting the Event and No Harassment During Events.

A9 ⚖️ Moderation Enforcement

  • You Must Accept The Escalation System via Moderation Policy Decisions and Cooperate With Staff.
  • Appeal System are allowed through designated channels and form.
  • No Evading Punishments, False Reports and Don't Arguing About Moderation in Public Channels.

A10 🏛 Final Authority

  • You Must Accept That ChillHub Management Team Have Final Say.
  • No Disputing Final Decisions Publicly, Refusing to Comply With Final Rulings and No Manipulating Rules to Challenge Decisions.

B | Policy Enforcement

B1 🛡️ Moderation Policy

  • This server follows a four-strike system: Verbal Warning → 24-Hour Mute → 3-Day Mute → Permanent Ban.
  • Severe violations or Discord Terms of Service breaches may result in an immediate permanent ban without warning.
  • All moderation actions are sent via DM.

B2 🤝🏼 Partnership Policy

  • Partnerships are only available for active servers with 50+ human members.
  • Must follow Discord's Terms of Service, maintain a positive community, and meet all partnership requirements.

B3 🔔 Social Notify Policy

  • Only TikTok, YouTube, and Twitch creators are eligible.
  • Accounts must have 50+ followers/subscribers, upload regularly, maintain an active audience, and comply with Discord's Terms of Service.

B4 🌟 VIP Policy

  • The VIP role is earned through selected events and giveaways.
  • VIP is a privilege, not an exemption from the rules.
  • Misuse of the role may result in its removal and further moderation action.

C | Guidelines Enforcement

C1 💡 Suggestions Post Guidelines

  • All suggestions must follow server rules.
  • Use proper tags and include a clear explanation.
  • Avoid hate speech and do not tag other users without their consent.

C2 🎨 Art Gallery Post Guidelines

  • All artwork must be appropriate and properly credited.
  • Post with the correct tags and share with permission.
  • Respect artists and server rules at all times.
